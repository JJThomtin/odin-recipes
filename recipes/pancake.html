Pancake

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 Cup Flour
  • 3 1/2 Teaspoons Baking Powder
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1 1/4 Cups Milk
  • 1 Tablespoon White Sugar
  • 3 Tablespoons Butter, Melted
  • 1 Eggs

Steps

    In a large bowl sift all dry ingredients (flour, baking powder, sugar, salt) and make a well in center
    Mix wet ingredients (milk, melted butter, eggs) until smooth
    Heat a lightly oiled pan over medium-high heat
    Use about 1/4 cup for each pancake
    Cook until bubbles form and edges are dry (2-3 minutes) and then flip until brown
