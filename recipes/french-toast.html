French Toast

Description

Easy to make french toast to start your day.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 Cup Milk
  • 6 Slices Brioche
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 1 Tablespoon Unsalted Butter
  • 1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract (Optional)
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Ground Cinnamon (Optional)

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Whisk eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, salt, milk in a bowl
  3. Lightly butter a skillet and heat over mediu-high heat
  4. Dunk bread in mixture, soaking both sides
  5. Place on hot skillet and cook until golden (Takes around 3-4 minutes per side)
