Waffle

Description

Your everyday easy to make waffle

Ingredients

  • 1/3 Cup Butter, Melted
  • 1 1/2 Cups Warm Milk
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 Tablespoons White Sugar
  • 4 Teaspoons Baking Powder
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt
  • 2 Cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Mix dry ingredients (flour, salt, baking powder, sugar) in a large bowl Preheat waffle iron
  3. Beat eggs and mix wet ingredients (milk, butter, vanilla extract) in a bowl
  4. Pour flour and milk mixture together and beat until blended
  5. Ladle the batter into the waffle iron
  6. Cook until golden and crisp
